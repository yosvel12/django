Questions and Answers

Who qualifies for Education Pricing?

Stormboard is proud to offer its software for free until July 31, 2016 as long as you meet these criteria.

  • For use in classroom education between teachers and students. (If you're using it for administrative work between faculty, we ask that you use our regular plans.)
  • Used in a formally accredited K-12 or higher education school. 
  • All education accounts must be registered using a valid school email address. 

If you want to use Stormboard for your faculty or administrative work or for profit teaching or facilitation work, please use our regular plans.

How can you thank us?

We'd appreciate you  tweeting and sharing Stormboard with your friends and colleagues of course!

Do you offer school or district pricing?

If you are an institution or educational district that is interested in discussing a full featured license, please contact us and we’ll get the conversation started.
